Investment Ecosystem Agent:
- General Idea:
The project aims to build an agentic AI system that models global market dynamics as a knowledge graph.
Agents continuously collect and analyze financial data and news to detect correlations, emerging trends, and investment opportunities.

Market: Tech companies in stock market
Output Data: List of Suggestions containing the prediction of the most likely future developments of target companies
Input Data: Datasets of Tech Companies and relevant markets with differents timestamps and price development
